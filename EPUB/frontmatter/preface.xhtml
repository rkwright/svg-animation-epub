Preface

This book is an exploration of the various methods available to animate SVG, including declarative SVG animation, JavaScript and CSS3 animation. These explorations have take place sporadically over almost 2 decades. The author, Ric Wright, is one of the original members of the SVG working group and a member of the team that developed the Adobe SVG Viewer (ASV) as well as the SVG authoring tools at Adobe. The author was in fact responsible for most of the import/export code at Adobe, including Illustrator and Indesign as well as LiveMotion (never shipped).

Ric is currently the Director of Engineering for the Readium project, which is an open-source foundation dedicated to providing web apps and a SDK for rendering EPUB, the standard format for eBooks. As a demonstration of the capabilities of EPUB and Readium, Ric took these examples and created this EPUB.

The details of how this book was created are covered in the colophon at the end of the book.

If you have any feedback, suggestions or bug reports about this book, you are strongly encouraged to contact Ric directly.
